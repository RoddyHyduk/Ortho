Payment

It is impossible to give an exact cost for treatment until we’ve had time to analyze the results of your initial examination and records. During your consultation with our team, we will review the exact cost of the projected treatment and various financial plans. We have many financing options available to accommodate your needs, and we will carefully review those all with you to determine a payment plan that’s best.

We are non-participatory in insurance companies, but we will fill out all necessary paperwork and forms and submit on behalf of our patients so they may obtain maximum reimbursement.

We work with all dental, orthodontic, and medical insurance policies. We are one of the most inclusive third-party offices working with medical insurance policies today.

Please let us know if you have any questions about your coverage or if you have other ideas regarding how we can make your entire orthodontic experience a pleasant, transparent one.
