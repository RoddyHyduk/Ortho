Braces and Aligners

The world of orthodontic care has seen innovation in the effectiveness, comfort, and aesthetics of appliance design. We are proud to offer a number of modern technologies allow us alternatives to heavy, dated appliances, opting for orthodontic appliances that cause little-to-no discomfort for our patients and look better than ever before.

Damon System® | Self-Ligating Braces

A popular brand of self-ligating braces, the Damon System utilizes a slide mechanism to hold the archwire in place instead of elastics, reducing the amount of pressure exerted on the teeth and allowing teeth to move more freely, quickly, and comfortably. Damon System treatment does not require the use of plastic or metal ties, and therefore requires fewer adjustments.

Benefits

  • No Tightening, Less Discomfort

  • Requires Fewer Appointments

  • Discreet, Comfortable, Sanitary

  • Available in Clear and Metal


Treatment

While required treatment times vary from patient-to-patient, the duration of treatment with the Damon System is typically much faster than with conventional braces (on average six months faster).

Invisalign® | Invisble Aligners

One of our most requested treatment types, Invisalign has revolutionized orthodontic care by providing a low-maintenance, aesthetically-preferred alternative to braces. Invisalign uses 3D computer-imaging technology to create a series of comfortable, clear and removable aligners that straighten your teeth without the hassle and appearance of braces.

Benefits

  • No dietary restrictions (remove to eat)

  • No additional brushing/flossing maintenance

  • Very comfortable, no abrasions

  • Near-invisible appearance


Treatment

Requires office visit only every 6-8 weeks. Total treatment time typically lasts 9-15 months with Invisalign, and the number of aligners worn during treatment is usually between 18 and 30, but both numbers will vary from case to case.

3M Incognito® | Lingual Braces

Clear Correct technology provides two unique benefits to both doctors and patients: whereas traditional orthodontics relies on plaster models of teeth, SureSmile provides orthodontists with a detailed 3D computer model of your mouth, allowing for higher precision when plotting tooth movement. Secondly, as opposed to an orthodontist bending the archwire for braces by hand, SureSmile custom-shapes each archwire with robot technology, allowing treatment to be more effective and comfortable.

We also offer Metal and Ceramic Braces, still the most popular types of orthodontic treatment in the world. Metal braces are smaller and more comfortable than ever before – using high-grade stainless steel brackets and arch wires. With metal braces, you have the option to select colored brackets and ties.

Ceramic and clear braces straighten teeth yet have more visual appeal. Made from either a clear or tooth-colored ceramic material, these types of braces are much less visible and therefore often preferred for their aesthetic advantage. However, non-metal braces do require more attention to diet and oral hygiene to maintain proper care.
