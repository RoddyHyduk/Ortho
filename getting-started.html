Getting Started

Becoming a new patient at Ortho is easy, and always starts wth a call to our office. Yet we want to go through here our comprehensive 3 step process to ensure we understand all facets of your unique case to provide the most accurate diagnosis. Here you will find descriptions about what to expect in each step of the process.

Ortho Troy

Step 1:
Your Complimentary Exam

Taking a Peek Under the Hood

Upon arriving at the office for your initial exam, you’ll check-in with Gina at the front desk and complete all necessary paperwork. (To save time, download the necessary forms here and fill them out prior to your visit.) Gina will escort you to the private examination room, where you’ll meet Dr. Hyduk for your initial exam. Parents are always welcome to accompany children for their initial exam.

During the 45-minute exam, Dr. Hyduk will thoroughly examine your teeth and begin to discuss potential treatment options available for you.

Step 2:
Complete Diagnostic Records

Assessing From All Angles

Your second visit to the office is reserved for the collection and analysis of complete diagnostic records, which often include plaster study models of your teeth, X rays, and digital photographs of your face and mouth. The information gathered through these exams is essential to developing a high-quality, customized treatment plan, in addition to tracking the progress of treatment as teeth begin to move under orthodontic care.

Step 3:
The Final Consultation

Deciding the Best Way Forward

With your exam and records complete, Dr. Hyduk and Gina will meet with you to thoroughly review your case and make a recommendation for how to proceed with treatment. Our goal for this meeting is to provide you with all the information you need to make an educated, confident decision about how to move forward with orthodontic care. At this time we will also review the various costs and payment plans available for the recommended treatment plan.

Once a direction for treatment is mutually agreed upon, you are now part of the Ortho family. Congrats! We’ll schedule an appointment to begin the installation of your appliance and your journey to a beautiful smile.

To get things started, give us a call at (248) 879-6200.
We’re happy to schedule your complimentary initial exam at a time that works best for you.
