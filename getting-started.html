Getting Started

Our new-patient process is easy, yet has multiple parts to ensure all facets of your unique case are understood before providing a diagnosis.

Below are brief descriptions about what to expect each step along the way.

Step 1: Complimentary Exam

Upon arriving at the office for your initial exam, you’ll check-in at the front desk and complete all necessary paperwork. (To save time, download the necessary forms here and fill them out prior to your visit.) You’ll be escorted to the private examination room where you’ll meet Dr. Hyduk for your initial exam. Parents are always welcome to accompany children for their initial exam.

During the 45-minute exam, Dr. Hyduk will thoroughly examine your teeth and begin to discuss potential treatment options available for you. At this time we can also provide an estimated cost for your treatment.

Ortho Troy

Step 2: Diagnostic Records

Your second visit to the office is reserved for the collection and analysis of complete diagnostic records, which often include plaster study models of your teeth, X rays, and digital photographs of your face and mouth. This appointment will take approximately 60 minutes. The information gathered through the review and evaluation of these records is essential to developing a high-quality, customized treatment plan, in addition to tracking the progress of treatment as the jaws and teeth begin to move under orthodontic care.

Step 3: Consultation

With your exam and records complete, we will meet with you to thoroughly review your case and make a recommendation for how to proceed with treatment. Our goal for this meeting is to provide you with all the information you need to confidently make a decision about how to move forward with orthodontic care. At this time we will also review the various costs and payment plans available for the recommended treatment plan.

Once a direction for treatment is mutually agreed upon, you are now part of the Ortho by Hyduk family. Congrats! We’ll schedule an appointment to begin the installation of your appliance and your journey to a healthy, balanced smile.

To get things started, give us a call at (248) 879-6200.
We’re happy to schedule your complimentary initial exam at a time that works best for you.
